frequently asked questions

RELATED TO THE COMMON ISSUES

  • FAQ

What Is The Per Sq. Ft. Charge?

buildAhome charges a basic price of Rs.1470* per Sq. Ft.

Is The Costing Final?

The amount charged is open for negotiation & may be decreased/increased based on the requirement & the design of the home.

What Costs Are Included?

All costs for construction including design & execution. The cost also includes finishing items like sanitary fixtures, doors & windows. See our packages here for a better understanding

Does buildAhome Charge Taxes?

Yes, buildAhome is a reputed organisation paying all statutory. The cost mentioned includes GST & no additional taxes are charged

Any Other Additional Charges?

There are no hidden charges in buildAhome. Download our detailed packages to understand better

Is There a Retention Amount?

buildAhome offers 3% of the total project cost as retention to our clients, payable upon handover only

Is There A Buffer-period In The Agreement If The Project Gets Delayed?

We provide a two month buffer period.

Does buildAhome Subcontract?

NEVER!!!

All the work done by buildAhome is handled and performed by the best-in-class in-house Architects & Civil Engineers.

Does buildAhome Help in Government Liason?

To the extent possible, buildAhome will help in getting necessary plans sanctioned along with other government approvals.

What Is The Schedule Of Payment?

All stages are divided into smaller stages of payments payable only after completion of a particular stage. These stages are clearly defined in the agreement even before signing of agreement

What is Structural Designing?

It is the designing of safe and economical specification of a structure, & to ensure that a planned structure will be sufficiently strong to carry out its purpose.

Why Do We Design?

The main objective of the Structural Design is to provide safety, stability and to improve the performance of the structure.

When the structural design is well done, it allows flexibility in architectural design.

Does buildAhome Assign Site Engineer To The Project?

Right from the initial kick-off each home construction will have a qualified Site Engineer.

What about Architecture?

Our Architecture Team & Design Team will be specifically working on your project to ensure top notch design.

Does buildAhome provide a 3D Top-View?

Yes, we do!

Will There Be Variation In The Drawings And Actual Execution?

No! What We Design, We Execute!

Are Different Designs & Elevations Accessible to View?

Yes! buildAhome provides

a) Over 600 elevations of homes
b) Over 1000 floor plans of various home sizes
c) Over 600 designs of windows, window grills, staircase railing

To help you understand the design process.

Do I Need To Check-in Regularly?

buildAhome provides regular check-ins so that you can be rest-assured that everything is going smoothly.

In addition, we have constructed many homes with clients living abroad who have come back only for their housewarming!

How Do I Receive A Status Update About The Construction?

  • buildAhome AppAn app which can accessed by the client in order to have a regular check on their work progress
  • WhatsAppA WhatsApp group is created which will include You, the Site Engineer assigned and every necessary person working on Your home
  • EmailA daily update is provided to You and any official authorization or order will be sent via e-mail for confirmation

What Is The buildAhome App?

  • buildAhome App is a platform which can be accessible by the team and the client
  • Here, the client can view the daily progress report along with images of the work carried out on site
  • Client can also have a check on work schedule, transaction status up to date
  • This app also provides the client to access the documents such as floor plans, working plans etc., shared by the team.

Is There Warranty?

  • We provide a 10 year Warranty on the Waterproofing.
  • Anti-Termite Treatment with minimum of 10 years of guarantee.
  • We provide 1 year construction warranty.

What is Curing?

It is the process of maintaining the moisture and temperature condition of concrete for hydration.

How long does the process of curing take?

Between 7 to 14 days.

Who Will Select Tile, Paint & Fixtures For My Home?

As it is Your home, you will have the final say on all things that will be used in Your home.

Type Of Wires & Switches Used?

  • Wire – Copper wire, Finolex or Polycab
  • Switches – Anchor/ Roma Switches

Why Are The Steel And Concrete Lofts Not Included?

Aesthetically steel and concrete lofts are obsolete, and thereby, not included.

However, if the clients desires for one, we shall provide it.

What Will Be The Grade Of Cement?

53 Grade – OPC & PPC

Further Questions?

We hope to have provided you answers for all initial queries which You may have.

If you need any further clarifications, please get in touch with our Team who will be happy to help!
